What is Being Proposed?

The Transportation Committee is recommending that Los Angeles establish parking restrictions for oversized vehicles on three street segments in Council District 11: Stoner Avenue (from Brookhaven to Sardis Avenue), Imlay Avenue (from Culver Drive to Utopia Avenue), and Lucerne Avenue (from Sepulveda Boulevard to Beloit Avenue). The restriction would prohibit vehicles exceeding 22 feet in length or 7 feet in height from parking on these streets between 2:00 a.m. and 6:00 a.m., with violators subject to towing.

Why?

While the document doesn't explicitly detail the rationale, these restrictions are authorized under Los Angeles Municipal Code Section 80.69.4 and California Vehicle Code Section 22507. The proposal appears aimed at addressing nighttime parking issues related to large commercial vehicles in residential neighborhoods during early morning hours when such vehicles would be most disruptive.

Key Details

Impact

Residents and businesses in CD 11 will be unable to park oversized vehicles overnight on these three street segments. The Committee submitted the measure without a recommendation, meaning the full City Council will make the final determination on adoption.

What is Being Proposed?

The Los Angeles City Council is adopting a resolution to prohibit parking of oversized vehicles (those exceeding 22 feet in length or 7 feet in height) during nighttime hours (2:00 a.m. to 6:00 a.m.) on three street segments in Council District 11. The Department of Transportation will post "tow away, no parking" signs at these locations to inform the public of the restriction.

Why?

The resolution addresses a growing problem in District 11 where large vehicles are frequently parked overnight on residential streets, obstructing travel lanes and creating dangerous traffic conditions. This action is authorized under California Vehicle Code Section 22507 and Los Angeles Municipal Code Section 80.69.4, which grant cities the authority to restrict oversized vehicle parking.

Key Details

The parking restrictions apply to both sides of three street segments: Stoner Avenue (Brookhaven Avenue to Sardis Avenue) Imlay Avenue (Culver Drive to Utopia Avenue) Lucerne Avenue (Sepulveda Boulevard to Beloit Avenue) Restrictions are in effect from 2:00 a.m. to 6:00 a.m. daily. The resolution was presented by Councilwoman Traci Park (District 11) and dated September 5, 2025.

Impact

Residents in the affected neighborhoods will see enforcement of overnight parking restrictions on large vehicles. The measure aims to improve traffic flow and public safety by clearing lanes during nighttime hours while still allowing oversized vehicle parking during daytime hours.
