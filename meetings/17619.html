REQUEST that the Housing Authority of the City of Los Angeles and LAHSA to report on the factors that affect vacancy rates in permanent supportive housing, including how the current acuity threshold impacts these rates, and how data shows, if at all, whether the recent point reduction in the acuity threshold resulted in higher occupancy rates and significantly shorter lease-up time periods. REQUEST LAHSA to report on the new matching system CESTTRR, including how it differs from the current matching prioritization system within CES.
